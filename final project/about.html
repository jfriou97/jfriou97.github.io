    Perinatal Quality Collaborative of North Carolina: We are here to make North Carolina the best place to give birth and be born. We happen to do this via easy to execute, low tech, increasingly minimal data intensive processes that require local leaders to believe they exist to make their hospital the best place to give birth and be born. We also happen to be free, fun, and meet multiple requirements your hospital is now being tasked with. Want to join us?
